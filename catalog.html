Welcome

Welcome to the ultimate hub for all thing computer science! We are dedicated to fueling your passion for tech.

In level 100 you'll be admitted as a Mathematical Science student. Based on your performance in Level 100, you would be given your desired course. We offer Single-Major courses, Combined-Major and a Major-Minor.

Single-Major

Here computer science is the main focus you can choose to add one extra course from a different department but is not compulsory. Our department has placed a premium on this cause meaning yould have to do extremly well in your level 100.

Combined-Major

With the Combined-Major, you can combine two major courses, for instance Computer Science and Mathematics, Computer Science and Statistics etc. There is also premium on this one .

Major-minor

With the Major-minor, you can choose to Major in the Computer Science or Minor in the Computer science. And ofcourse there is premium on choosing to major in the Computer Science and minor in a different department.
