Welcome to our world. We are a passionate team of individuals who believe in the power of creativity and innovation. Our mission is to bring you the best experiences through our products and services. With a shared love for technology and a drive to make a difference, we work tirelessly to create solutions that make your life easier and more enjoyable. We're dedicated to customer satisfaction and strive to exceed your expectations every step of the way.

Our main goal is to provide students with a solid foundation in computer science and prepare them for future careers in technology. We offer a wide range of courses that cover programming, algorithms, data structures, software development, and more. Our department is dedicated to creating a supportive and engaging learning environment where students can explore their interests and develop their skills. We also organize workshops to encourage students to apply their knowledge and collaborate with their peers. We believe that computer science is not just about coding but also about problem-solving, critical thinking, and creativity. We strive to empower our students to become innovative thinkers and leaders in the field.

Our Organization

The University of Ghana is one of the Africa's most preeminent public universities. Our impact on individuals, our region and the world is profound. whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting the grand challenges of our time through dauntless research. UG educates more than 38,000students annually, with approximately 2.64% being computer science students. We turn ideas into impact and transform the world around us. REGISTRATION

The Department of Computer Science is under the Scool of Physical and Mathematical Sciences

The School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences (SPMS) is one of the constituent units of the newly created College of Basic and Applied Sciences. Its mandate is to provide quality science education through teaching, research and dissemination of research findings. The School is headed by a Dean whose mandate is to provide academic and administrative leadership to the School.

Students who wish to offer Computer Science ar required to select Mathematical Science as their prefered course. then based on the individuals performance in level 100, it would be decided upon whether or not you are fit for the course
