GENERAL FAQs

  1. What programs does the Computer Science Department offer?

    The Computer Science department offers short certificate courses, undergraduate and post graduate courses such as BSc.Computer Science,MSc.Computer Science,MPhil Computer Science and P.H.D Computer Science.

  2. What research areas are covered in the Computer Science Department?

    The department covers a wide range of research areas, including artificial intelligence,machine learning,data science, cybersecurity, software engineering and more.Visit the research page for detailed information.

  3. Can I combine major in Computer Science and other fields?

    Yes it is possible to combine major in Computer Science and another field.Consult with your academic advisor to plan your course schedule and ensure you meet the requirements for both majors.

  4. How can I get involved in Research Projects ?

    Students interested in research can explore opportunities by contacting faculty members,participating in research seminars,and exploring ongoing projects. Many professors also welcome undergraduate and graduate students to join their research teams.

  5. Are there opportunities for internships or co-op programs ?

    Yes, our department collaborates with industry partners to provide internships opportunities for students.We also have dedicated career services office to assist students in securing practical experience on the field.
