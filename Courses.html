SHORT COURSES

Certificate Courses

Whether you are looking to enhance your existing skills or explore a new area of interest, our certificate courses offer a flexible and accessible learning experience.Below is a list of the short courses offered:

UNDERGRADUATE COURSES

Become a world-class software engineer or IT professional by taking the 4-year intensive and practice based degree program in Computer Science.

The journey begins with an introduction to the fundamentals of Computer Science for both IT and Computer Science majors or minors. IT students offer an elective DCIT 105 to and Computer Science students get to offer additional Maths electives such as Math 121, Math 123 and Math 126 to boost their problem-solving thinking ability. Some courses offered by both IT and CS majors include:

  • Introduction to Computer Science(DCIT 101)
  • Computer Hardware Fundamentals and Circuits (DCIT 102)
  • Office Productivity Tools(DCIT 103)
  • Programming Fundamentals(DCIT 104)
  • Maths 122(Calculus I)

Second year of the BSc.Computer Science offers a variety of courses which shape you to become an expert in various fields in Computer Science with internships to gain experience and field knowledge. Some of the courses are:

  • Internship(DCIT 200)
  • Programming I(DCIT 201)
  • Mobile App Development(DCIT 202)
  • Digital Circuits and Logic Systems Design(DCIT 203)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms(DCIT 204)
  • System Administration(DCIT 206)
  • Computer Architecture and Organization (DCIT 207)
  • Software Engineering(DCIT 210)

Delve deeper into Computer Science and gain insight on the inner workings of a computer and its components by offering courses like

  • Operating Systems(DCIT 301)
  • Computer Networks(DCIT 303)
  • Database Fundamentals(DCIT 305)
  • Digital Circuits and Logic Systems Design(DCIT 203)
  • Mini-Project(DCIT 307)
  • Embedded Systems and IoT(DCIT 309)
  • Machine Learning (DCIT 311)
  • Introduction to Artificial Intelligence(DCIT 313)

Take the final step in mastering your craft in Computer Science and IT and showcase your skill-set to the tech world but before you go,there is a compulsory project.

  • Project(DCIT 400)
  • Management Principles in Computing(DCIT 402)
  • Advanced Databases(DCIT 404)
  • Advanced Computer Networks(DCIT 406)
  • Compilers(DCIT 408)
  • Digital Signal Processing(DCIT 416)
  • Systems and Network Security(DCIT 418)
  • Wireless Systems and Networks(DCIT 428)
